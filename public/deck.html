The idea

Vibe coding is building by describing.

You say what you want in plain words. The AI writes the code. You run it, see what happened, and steer. You do not learn to code first. You build first, and pick things up as you go.

Term coined by Andrej Karpathy, 2025

The loop

Describe.
Build. Run. Steer.

Repeat until it does what you actually meant. Most of the work is the steering.
